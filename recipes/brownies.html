Brownies

Description

Easy Brownies to make with a short ingredient list!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Spread the batter into a prepared pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is dry.
  4. Cut into squares.
